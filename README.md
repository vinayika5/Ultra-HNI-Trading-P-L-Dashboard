## 🗂️ Project Overview

This project involved building an end-to-end interactive trading analytics dashboard covering **5 segments** of financial markets:

- Equity Cash
- Equity Derivatives (Futures)
- Equity Options
- Currency Futures
- Commodity Futures

The goal was to go beyond numbers — delivering **clarity, context, and actionable direction** to an HNI investor managing a ₹121.84bn portfolio.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Consolidate multi-segment trading data into a single unified report
- Track P&L performance — Gross, Net, Segment-wise and Month-wise
- Identify hidden cost leakages across brokerage, STT, GST and other charges
- Surface actionable insights for tax optimization and portfolio rebalancing
- Deliver an HNI-grade interactive dashboard with professional UI/UX

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

The dashboard is structured across **6 interactive tabs:**

**1. Overview**
- Trading P&L Contribution by Segment (Donut)
- Total Turnover by Segment (Donut)

**2. Segment-wise Trading P&L**
- Segment-wise Gross P&L (Bar)
- Segment-wise Charges (Bar)
- Segment-wise Net P&L (Bar)

**3. Monthly Trend**
- Monthly Net P&L — All Segments Combined (Line Chart with Quarter markers)

**4. Top Stocks**
- Top 10 Profitable Stocks
- Lowest 10 P&L Stocks

**5. Charges Breakdown**
- Total Charges Breakdown — STT, Brokerage, GST, Stamp Duty, Exchange Txn, SEBI (Stacked Bar)

**6. P&L Heatmap**
- Monthly P&L Heatmap — Month × Segment Matrix (Conditional Formatting)

---


## 🔍 Key Insights

- **Equity Cash = 79.35%** of total P&L — high concentration risk identified and flagged
- **STT = 70.2%** of all charges — single biggest cost leakage, LTCG optimization recommended
- **Currency Futures** = Most cost-efficient segment — 4x better charge ratio than Equity Cash
- **April 2024 = ₹0.69bn → March 2025 = ₹27.05bn** — 39x growth within a single FY
- **100% Win Rate** across 850 trades — extraordinary risk management
- **Brokerage renegotiation** opportunity = potential annual saving identified
